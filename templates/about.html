ABOUT US

MoMA - this is a space that unites the creativity of the past and the present. We have our own collection, collected from all over the world.

Discover Modern Art

The Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) is one of the most iconic museums in the world, dedicated to modern and contemporary art. Established in 1929 in New York City, MoMA has become a symbol of creativity and innovation, attracting millions of visitors every year. The museum features an extensive collection of paintings, sculptures, photography, architecture, film, and digital art from internationally renowned artists.

A Space for Inspiration

MoMA's mission is to inspire and educate the public through its exhibitions, educational programs, and research initiatives. The museum is committed to showcasing diverse perspectives and fostering a deeper understanding of modern art's impact on society. With its dynamic programming and commitment to accessibility, MoMA continues to be a vital cultural institution that celebrates the transformative power of art.
